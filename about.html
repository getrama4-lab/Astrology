About Astrologer Sunitha Yagnamurthy

Sunitha Yagnamurthy is a Hyderabad-based astrologer and spiritual content creator who provides astrological consultations and spiritual insights.

Services and Content

She runs "Sri Gayatri Jyotishyalam" and offers personalized, detailed astrological predictions to her clients. Clients can reach her for consultations directly, and she focuses on sharing knowledge about Muhurtas (auspicious timings) and remedies for health or life issues.

Online Presence

Sunitha shares spiritual and astrological content actively across multiple social media platforms:

  • YouTube: Her channel, "SUNITHA YAGNAMURTHY Vlog", features videos on Hindu mythology, historical figures (like Janaka Maharaja), and devotional recitations such as the Kanakadhara Stotram and Vishnu Sahasranama. She also discusses specific astrological topics like Nakshatras, diseases, and Tantric remedies.
  • Instagram: On her profile @sunitha.yagnamurthy, she shares daily horoscopes, spiritual reels, and contact information for her astrology services.
